SERVICES - WE-CARE HOSPITAL & RESEARCH CENTER

Neurology

Our Neurology department specializes in diagnosing and treating disorders of the nervous system. Our team of neurologists provides expert care for conditions such as epilepsy, migraines, multiple sclerosis, and more.

Urology

The Urology department at We-Care Hospital offers comprehensive urological services for both men and women. Our urologists are skilled in treating kidney stones, urinary tract infections, prostate issues, and other urological conditions.

Gynecology

Our Gynecology department provides personalized care for women's reproductive health. From routine check-ups to specialized treatments, our gynecologists ensure the well-being of our female patients.

Cardiology

Our Cardiology department offers advanced cardiac care and treatments. Our team of cardiologists is dedicated to diagnosing and treating heart conditions, ensuring optimal heart health for our patients.

Orthopedics

The Orthopedics department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of musculoskeletal conditions and injuries. Our orthopedic surgeons provide comprehensive care for joint problems, fractures, and sports injuries.

Pediatrics

Our Pediatrics department offers specialized care for infants, children, and adolescents. Our pediatricians provide preventive care, vaccinations, and treatment for various pediatric conditions to ensure the health and well-being of young patients.
